# Section 11 --- Known Dislikes, Anti-Patterns, and Constraints

- **\[Preference\]** Never wants Windows instructions or keybindings
    unless explicitly requested.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers concise, high-signal answers by default.
- **\[Preference\]** Dislikes buzzword-heavy or marketing-heavy
    explanations.
- **\[Preference\]** Strong dislike of click-heavy workflows and
    opaque systems.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers Markdown rather than HTML examples.
- **\[Preference\]** Strong preference for keyboard-first, scriptable
    systems.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers honest disagreement and factual grounding
    over agreeableness.
- **\[Preference\]** Wants explanations of why something cannot work
    when constraints apply.
- **\[Preference\]** Does not want org-mode recommendations unless
    explicitly requested.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers reproducible workflows and inspectable
    state over magic abstractions.
- **\[Inference\]** Quickly loses trust in systems that obscure
    mechanics or remove observability.
