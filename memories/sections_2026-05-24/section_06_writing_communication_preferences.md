# Section 6 --- Writing, Communication, and Response Preferences

- **\[Preference\]** Prefers short, signal-dense answers by default.
- **\[Preference\]** Will ask for more detail when wanted.
- **\[Preference\]** Values honesty over agreeableness.
- **\[Preference\]** Dislikes flattery, filler, buzzword fog, and
    vague abstractions.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers explanations of *why something is
    impossible* when constraints exist.
- **\[Preference\]** Prefers Markdown over HTML examples.
- **\[Preference\]** Never wants Windows instructions or keybindings
    unless explicitly requested.
- **\[Preference\]** Likes compressed detail density and practical
    guidance.
- **\[Preference\]** Enjoys metaphors when grounded and technically
    relevant.
- **\[Inference\]** Tends to evaluate communication by
    usefulness-to-action rather than polish.
